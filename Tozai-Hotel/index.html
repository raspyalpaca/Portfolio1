Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Tozai Hotel offers a special lodging experience on the California North Coast with Panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at Tozai Hotel includes
a sumptuously appointed private yurt and a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Unwind in the heated pool and whirlpool
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours
  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying complimentary appetizers and beverages
  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view
